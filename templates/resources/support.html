Common Troubleshooting Steps

CSV Import Issues
  1. Ensure your CSV has date and price columns
  2. Check that dates are in YYYY-MM-DD format
  3. Verify that price values are numerical
  4. Remove any special characters or formatting
  5. Keep file size under 10MB
Chart Display Problems
  1. Clear your browser cache
  2. Disable browser extensions that might interfere
  3. Try a different browser
  4. Ensure JavaScript is enabled
  5. Check your internet connection
Technical Support

If you're experiencing persistent technical issues, please contact our technical support team at tech-support@cycletrader.com with a description of your problem, screenshots if applicable, and your system information.
